Odin's Chicken curry

This pasta sauce is excellent in lasagna or as a meat sauce. I use it in my popular lasagna recipe, so I thought I'd share it on its own.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • cream sauce
  • curry
  • pepper
  • salt

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Cut chicken into little stripes or dices.

Step 3

Season the cut chicken with salt and pepper.

Step 4

Put it into an hot pan and roast it gently.

Step 5

Once the flesh is white, add the cream sauce and season it with curry. If necessary add salt and pepper once more.

Step 6

Serve with your favorite rice and enjoy!
