Odin's Pasta

So good with Pasta! And on top of that it is rich in Protein and healthy.

Ingredients

  • garlic
  • onions
  • minced meat
  • Tomato paste
  • tomato puree
  • Beef broth
  • cocktail tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Italian seasoning
  • oregano
  • salt
  • mixed pepper

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients.

Step 2

Dice the onions and the garlic.

Step 3

Throw the diced ingredients together with the minced meat in an pan and roast ist gently.

Step 4

Once the meat ist brown, give the tomato paste together with the tomato puree and the Beef broth into the Pan.

Step 5

Season it with pepper, salt, oregano and italian seasoning and let it simmer for a couple minutes.

Step 6

Serve with your favorite pasta and enjoy!
